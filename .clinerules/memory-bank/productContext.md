# Product Context

## Why SurveillanceHub exists
SurveillanceHub aims to solve the problem of managing surveillance infrastructure across multiple store locations. Businesses with several retail outlets or facilities often struggle with a centralized system to keep track of their cameras and the stores they are assigned to. This leads to inefficiencies in maintenance, monitoring, and auditing.

## Problems it solves
- **Decentralized Camera Management**: Eliminates the need to manually track cameras per store using spreadsheets or disparate systems.
- **Lack of Overview**: Provides a central dashboard for quick insights into store and camera status.
- **Inefficient CRUD Operations**: Streamlines the process of adding, updating, and deleting store and camera records.

## How it should work
Users should be able to log in and see a dashboard providing a high-level overview. From there, they can navigate to dedicated sections for "Shops" and "Cameras" to perform full CRUD operations. Each camera should be linkable to a specific store.

## User Experience Goals
- **Intuitive Interface**: Easy to navigate and understand, even for non-technical users.
- **Fast Operations**: Quick response times for all CRUD actions.
- **Clear Feedback**: Users should always receive clear feedback on the success or failure of their actions (e.g., flash messages).
